# Privacy Policy for EmojiTap

**Last Updated: February 8, 2026**

## Overview

EmojiTap ("the App") is developed by NomadJackalope (Damon Eli DeWaide). This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

### Data Stored Locally
All game progress and settings are stored **locally on your device only**. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external servers.

This includes:
- Game progress (tokens, upgrades, tower positions)
- Sound preferences

### Advertising
The App uses Google AdMob to display **non-personalized advertisements only**. We do not use App Tracking Transparency (ATT) and do not track users. AdMob may collect limited data for non-personalized ad delivery, including:
- IP address
- General location (country/region)

We do not collect or share device identifiers for advertising purposes.

For more information on how Google uses data, visit: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

## Data Storage

Your game data never leaves your device. If you delete the app, all local data is permanently deleted.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
