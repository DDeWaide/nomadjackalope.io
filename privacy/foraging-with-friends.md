# Privacy Policy for Foraging with Friends

**Last Updated: February 1, 2026**

## Overview

Foraging with Friends ("the App") is developed by NomadJackalope (Damon Eli DeWaide). This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

### Location Data
The App uses your device's location to help you mark where you discover forageable items. Location data is stored **locally on your device only**. We do not collect, transmit, or store your location data on any external servers.

### Photos
If you choose to attach photos to your foraging finds, these are stored **locally on your device only**. We do not upload or access your photos.

### Data Stored Locally
All foraging data you create is stored locally on your device only, including:
- Marked locations
- Photos you attach
- Notes and descriptions

### Advertising
The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data to provide personalized ads. This may include:
- Device identifiers
- IP address
- General location (country/region)
- App usage data

You can opt out of personalized advertising in your device settings.

For more information on how Google uses data, visit: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except as required for advertising services described above.

## Data Storage

Your foraging data never leaves your device. If you delete the app, all local data is permanently deleted.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
