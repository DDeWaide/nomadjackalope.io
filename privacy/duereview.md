# Privacy Policy for DueReview

**Last Updated: February 6, 2026**

## Overview

DueReview is developed by NomadJackalope (Damon Eli DeWaide). This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

### Data Stored Locally

All deadline items, reminders, costs, and settings you create are stored locally on your device only. We do not collect, transmit, or store your personal data on any external servers.

This includes:
- Item names and categories
- Expiration dates and reminder preferences
- Cost estimates and notes
- Usage statistics

### Advertising

The app displays non-personalized advertisements served by Google AdMob. We do not use App Tracking Transparency (ATT) and do not collect or share your device advertising identifier (IDFA).

AdMob may collect limited technical data to serve non-personalized ads, including general device information and ad interaction data. This data is not linked to your identity.

For more information about Google's data practices, visit: [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties. We do not track users across apps or websites.

## Data Security

Your data is stored locally on your device and protected by your device's built-in security features. We do not have access to your data.

## Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app is intended for general audiences.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at support@nomadjackalope.io.
